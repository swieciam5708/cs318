About Us

Welcome to the website for the Office Club at UW Eau Claire!

This club is centered around the hit TV show The Office. This club is a place to come once a month to take a break from the stress of school, have some fun, and make new friends. At our meetings we play fun games based off the show. There are Schrute Bucks given to winners and they are redeemable for prizes! We host watch parties and sell Dunder Mifflin tshirts. If you love the office as much as we do, what are you waiting for?
